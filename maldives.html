Maldives

This beautiful string of coral islands in the Indian Ocean must surely qualify as being an earthly paradise and most visitors to the islands enjoy a warm welcome and experience a true feeling of peace and tranquillity. These islands, however, are noted for their stormy past both physically and politically and even in recent years, there has been much unrest.
