Turkey

Turkey has a collectivist culture whereby strong loyalty is shown to familial and social groups, as well as the broader nation. People's relationships with their neighbours and community are generally closer than what many from the English-speaking West experience.

Cultural life

To outline Turkish culture and customs in a single article is unimaginable in light of the fact that the decent variety of legacy the nation over fluctuates and different societies, for example, Armenian, Arabic, Georgian, and Greek practices have been woven in since forever.
